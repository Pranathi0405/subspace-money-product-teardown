# Final Recommendations

Based on my exploration of Subspace.money, I believe the platform has a strong concept and a modern user interface. However, several improvements can make the experience more user-friendly and increase user trust.

## Top Recommendations

### 1. Clearly Explain the Product

The platform should immediately explain:

- What the product does
- How it works
- Who it is designed for
- How users benefit from it

A simple introduction section on the homepage would help first-time users.

### 2. Improve Onboarding

Provide a short walkthrough or tutorial that explains:

- How to get started
- How subscriptions are shared
- How users can save money

This will reduce confusion for new users.

### 3. Use User-Friendly Error Messages

Technical system errors should not be shown directly to users.

Instead, provide simple explanations and suggested actions to solve problems.

### 4. Add More Popular Brands

Including additional popular services such as Disney+ Hotstar and other major subscription platforms would increase platform value.

### 5. Build User Trust

Users are careful before spending money online.

The platform can increase trust by:

- Showing customer reviews
- Displaying security information
- Highlighting successful user experiences
- Providing transparent pricing information

### 6. Consider a Free Trial

A short free trial period can help users understand the platform before making a payment decision.

## Conclusion

Subspace.money already provides a good foundation with an attractive interface and useful concept. By improving product clarity, onboarding, trust-building, and service coverage, the platform can provide a significantly better experience for new users and increase long-term engagement.
