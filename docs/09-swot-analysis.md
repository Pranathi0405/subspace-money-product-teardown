# SWOT Analysis

## Strengths

- Modern and attractive user interface
- Multiple categories available in one platform
- Convenient discovery of subscriptions and services
- Good concept for saving money through shared subscriptions
- User-friendly navigation in most sections

## Weaknesses

- Product purpose is not immediately clear for first-time users
- Limited onboarding guidance
- Technical error messages are shown directly to users
- Some popular brands and services are missing
- Users may need additional trust signals before making payments

## Opportunities

- Add more popular brands and subscription services
- Introduce onboarding tutorials and walkthroughs
- Offer free trials for new users
- Improve trust through testimonials and success stories
- Expand partnerships with subscription providers

## Threats

- Competition from established subscription-sharing platforms
- User concerns regarding payment security
- Difficulty convincing first-time users to trust a new platform
- Rapid changes in subscription service policies

## Summary

Subspace.money has a strong foundation with a good product concept and attractive design. By improving product clarity, onboarding, trust-building, and service coverage, the platform can significantly improve user adoption and retention.
