# First Impression

## Overview

When I first explored Subspace.money, I found the platform visually appealing and user-friendly. The platform brings together different categories such as entertainment, food, shopping, and subscriptions in one place.

My initial impression was similar to visiting a mall where multiple services are available under one roof. Instead of opening multiple apps individually, users can discover services from a single platform.

## What I Understood

From my exploration, I understood that Subspace.money helps users discover, access, and manage various subscription-based services from a centralized platform.

The idea of having multiple services available in one place can help users save time and compare options more easily.

## My Initial Experience

The user interface felt modern and easy to navigate. The categories were clearly visible, and the platform looked trustworthy from a design perspective.

However, as a first-time user, I found it difficult to immediately understand:

* What exactly the platform does
* How it works
* How it differs from other available solutions
* What actions a new user should take first

## Personal Observation

I believe users who are already familiar with digital subscriptions may understand the platform more quickly.

However, users with little knowledge of subscription-sharing platforms may need additional guidance to understand the product's value proposition.
