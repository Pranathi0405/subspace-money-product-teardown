# Product Feedback 3: Trust and Credibility

## Observed

While exploring the platform, I understood that users can access subscription-based services and potentially save money through shared subscriptions.

The concept is useful and attractive, especially for users who regularly pay for multiple digital services.

However, as a first-time visitor, I did not immediately feel confident enough to make a payment or join a subscription.

## Problem

Users are naturally cautious when spending money online.

Before purchasing a subscription or sharing costs with others, users need confidence that:

* The platform is legitimate
* Their payments are secure
* The subscription process is reliable
* Other users have successfully used the service

At present, the trust-building elements are not immediately visible to new users.

## Why It Matters

Trust is one of the most important factors in any platform that involves payments.

Even if users like the concept, they may hesitate to continue if they are unsure about the platform's reliability.

This can impact conversion rates and user growth.

## Ship Instead

I would strengthen trust by introducing:

1. Customer testimonials and reviews
2. Success stories from real users
3. Clear explanation of how subscription sharing works
4. Security and payment assurance badges
5. Transparent refund and support information

## Expected Impact

Users would feel more confident about making payments and joining subscriptions.

This would improve conversion rates and increase trust in the platform.
