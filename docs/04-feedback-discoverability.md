# Product Feedback 2: Discoverability

## Observed

While exploring the platform, I noticed that there are many apps and services available across different categories.

The variety is useful because users can discover multiple subscriptions from a single platform.

However, finding a specific service can sometimes be difficult when there are many options available.

During my exploration, I searched for Hotstar but could not find it.

## Problem

As the number of apps grows, users may spend more time searching for a specific service.

This can create friction in the discovery experience and reduce the efficiency of the platform.

Users who visit the platform with a specific service in mind may become frustrated if they cannot quickly find what they are looking for.

## Why It Matters

One of the key promises of a subscription discovery platform is convenience.

If users cannot quickly discover services, the platform may feel more time-consuming than expected.

This can negatively impact engagement and user satisfaction.

## Ship Instead

I would improve discoverability through:

1. Enhanced search functionality
2. Popular apps section
3. Trending subscriptions section
4. Better category filtering
5. "App not found?" recommendation feature

These improvements would help users locate services more efficiently.

## Expected Impact

Users would spend less time searching and more time exploring available subscriptions.

This would improve overall user experience and increase platform engagement.
