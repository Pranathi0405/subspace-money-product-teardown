# Target Users Analysis

## Primary User Segments

Based on my exploration of Subspace.money, I believe the platform is primarily designed for users who frequently use digital services, subscriptions, and online platforms.

### 1. Gen Z and College Students

I believe Gen Z users are one of the strongest target audiences for this platform.

They actively use:

* OTT subscriptions
* Food delivery apps
* Shopping platforms
* Music streaming services
* Online memberships

Having multiple services available in one place can help them discover and manage subscriptions more efficiently.

### 2. Working Professionals

Working professionals often have limited time to compare services across multiple platforms.

A centralized platform can help them:

* Explore subscriptions faster
* Compare available options
* Reduce the effort of switching between multiple applications

### 3. Families

Families that use multiple digital subscriptions may benefit from having services organized in a single platform.

This can improve visibility and make subscription management easier.

## My Perspective

While exploring the platform, it reminded me of visiting a shopping mall where different stores are available under one roof.

In a similar way, Subspace.money brings together different categories such as entertainment, food, shopping, and subscriptions into a single platform.

Among all user groups, I believe Gen Z users would benefit the most because they actively engage with digital platforms and are more likely to explore multiple subscriptions and online services.
