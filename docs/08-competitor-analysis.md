# Competitor Analysis

## Competitors Considered

- Spliiit
- Together Price
- Sharesub

## Observations

I explored a few subscription-sharing platforms to understand how they present their services and onboard users.

Most competitor platforms immediately explain:

- What the platform does
- How subscription sharing works
- How users can save money
- Why the platform is trustworthy

These platforms focus heavily on educating first-time users before asking them to sign up.

## Comparison with Subspace.money

### Strengths of Subspace.money

- Modern and attractive user interface
- Multiple categories available in one place
- Easy discovery of different services
- Good concept for subscription management

### Areas for Improvement

- Product purpose is not immediately clear
- Onboarding guidance is limited
- Trust-building information could be more visible
- Some popular brands and services are missing

## Key Learning

Competitors focus strongly on clarity and trust before asking users to take action.

Subspace.money already has a strong design foundation and can become more competitive by improving communication, onboarding, and user education.

## Conclusion

Subspace.money has a strong concept and user interface. By improving clarity, trust, and service coverage, it can provide an even better experience for new users.
