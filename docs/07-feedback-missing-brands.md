# Product Feedback 5: Missing Brands and Services

## Observed

While exploring different categories on the platform, I searched for some popular services and brands.

I noticed that certain well-known platforms were not available in the search results. For example, I was unable to find Disney+ Hotstar.

## Problem

Users expect a platform like Subspace.money to include most major and commonly used subscription services.

When popular brands are missing, users may feel that the platform is incomplete and may stop searching for additional services.

This can reduce the overall value users receive from the platform.

## Recommendation

- Expand the catalog of supported brands and services.
- Continuously add popular subscription platforms based on user demand.
- Allow users to suggest missing brands directly through the platform.
- Display upcoming brands that are planned for future addition.

## Expected Outcome

Adding more popular brands and services will improve platform completeness, increase user engagement, and encourage users to return regularly.
