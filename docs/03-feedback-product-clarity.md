# Product Feedback 1: Product Clarity
## Observed

While exploring Subspace.money, I noticed that the platform contains multiple categories, subscriptions, and services. The user interface is visually appealing and easy to navigate.

However, as a first-time user, I was unable to immediately understand the core purpose of the platform.

## Problem

The platform does not clearly communicate:

* What the product does
* How it works
* Who it is designed for
* How it is different from other available platforms

A user who is already familiar with subscription-sharing platforms may understand the product after some exploration.

However, users with little or no prior knowledge may feel confused during their first visit.

## Why It Matters

Users generally make decisions within the first few minutes of visiting a website.

If the value proposition is not immediately clear, users may leave the platform before exploring its features.

This can affect user acquisition, engagement, and conversion rates.

## Ship Instead

I would introduce a dedicated "How It Works" section near the top of the homepage.

The section should explain:

1. What Subspace.money does
2. How users benefit from it
3. How subscriptions are managed
4. Why users should choose this platform over alternatives

A short visual walkthrough or onboarding flow could further improve understanding.

## Expected Impact

New users would understand the product faster, gain confidence in the platform, and be more likely to continue exploring available services.
